How It Works

Simplified parcel delivery at your fingertips—whether you’re sending or delivering.

1. Sign Up

Create an account as a customer or register as an “Eagle” driver. Our seamless registration process gets you started in minutes.

2. Book or Accept Deliveries

Customers can schedule pickups and deliveries, while our freelance drivers receive delivery notifications on the go.

3. Real-Time Tracking

Follow your parcel’s journey live through our app. Enjoy transparency and peace of mind from pickup to drop-off.

4. Delivery & Feedback

Once delivered, rate your experience and help us continually refine our service. We value your trust and feedback.
